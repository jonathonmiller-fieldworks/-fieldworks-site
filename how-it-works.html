Real feedback. Real fixes.

One of our own crew flagged the app quietly deducting a lunch break even when "no lunch taken" was marked. Fixed same day — because there was no ticket to file, just a text to the person who built it.

Payroll exports, office reporting, the quiet back-office work that keeps everyone in sync — we build for those moments too, not just the parts your crew sees in the field.
